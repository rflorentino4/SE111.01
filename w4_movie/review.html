REVIEW of the series Gintama (2005)

Romina Florentino January 31st, 2024

Watch Link

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

It took me about a year to finish Gintama just because I'm a lazy watcher. It is safe to say, however, that it is my favorite anime to date. The combination of humor, lessons on friendship, and a slice of seriousness, make for the perfect anime in my eyes. This show has carried me through many cold nights, and I love recommending it to anyone willing to listen. It's a great starter anime because there's never a dull moment, seeing as after every "serious episode," there's a montage of comedy waiting.

Trivia for Gintama

  • Kagura was supposed to be much taller
  • Gintama was intended to be a serious samurai manga
  • Gintama is a pun on the word "Kintama," which means "testicles" in Japanese
