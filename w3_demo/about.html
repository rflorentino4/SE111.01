✩ ABOUT ✩

I am 17 years old and a Cybersecurity major. I live at home with my mom and my older brother. We have three pets: 2 cats and a ferret. I love listening to music, at all points of the day. I feel like music is magical; it has healing powers. I'm currently going down the "indie-rock" rabbit hole, they've been playing it nonstop at my job and some of these songs are wonderful. For the most part, though, I listen to a lot of latin and rap music.

I love playing video games as a fun pastime. In particular, I enjoy playing shooter and co-op (team) games. March officially marks one year since I've started going to the gym. It's allowed me to get closer with my brother which I'm incredibly grateful for. It's also helped me work on my mental health, something I needed to start prioritising.
I can speak two languages: English and Spanish! My main inspiration in life is my mother, I owe it all to her.
